Priya is from Kolkata's South 24 Parganas — Bengali by roots, warm by nature, and someone who takes both her festivals and her exams seriously.

Since finishing her Arts degree at the University of Calcutta, she's been preparing for the Railway Recruitment Board exam, chasing the kind of stability that makes a whole family proud.

Off duty, she's the one twirling in a saree at a wedding, or walking a quiet riverside path in a bright pink jacket — equal parts festive and grounded.
